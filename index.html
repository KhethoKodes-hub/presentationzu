Scar 01

Chronic Stress

Not the normal pressure before a deadline. This is a constant, grinding state of tension that never fully goes away.

  • Mental & physical exhaustion
  • Disturbed sleep & appetite changes
  • Constant feeling of overwhelm
  • Short temper, withdrawal from people
06 / 17
Scar 02

Imposter
Syndrome

"

I don't deserve to be here. Everyone else is smarter than me.

  • Chronic feeling of being a fraud
  • Downplaying your own achievements
  • Fear of being "found out"
07 / 17
Scar 03

Performance
Anxiety

The body goes into fight-or-flight mode — not for survival, but for a test paper.

  • Panic attacks before exams
  • Fear of public speaking & presenting
  • Blanking out under pressure
  • Avoiding opportunities due to fear
08 / 17
Scar 04

Spiritual Guilt

When mental health struggles are mistaken for weak faith — creating a double burden.

  • "If I just prayed more, I'd be fine"
  • Shame around seeking therapy
  • Hiding struggles from faith communities

Mental struggle is not a sign of weak faith. God designed the mind — and healers.

09 / 17
Scar 05

Quiet
Depression

They attend every lecture. They laugh at lunch. They respond to messages. But inside — nothing.

  • Smiling on the outside, hollow within
  • Losing interest in things once loved
  • Exhausted by simply existing
  • Functioning — but not truly living
10 / 17
Scar 06

Functional
Burnout

Still submitting assignments. Still leading the group. Still showing up. But running on fumes and willpower alone.

  • Achieving while completely depleted
  • Numbness disguised as productivity
  • No joy in accomplishments
  • Dread for each new day
