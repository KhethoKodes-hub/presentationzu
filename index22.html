Scar 01

Chronic Stress

Not the normal pressure before a deadline. A constant, grinding state of tension that never fully releases.

  • Mental & physical exhaustion
  • Disrupted sleep & appetite
  • Permanent feeling of overwhelm
  • Short temper, withdrawal from people

Scar 03

Performance
Anxiety

The body enters fight-or-flight mode — not for survival, but for a test paper or presentation.

  • Panic attacks before exams
  • Blanking out completely under pressure
  • Fear of presentations & public speaking
  • Avoiding opportunities because of fear

Scar 04

Spiritual Guilt

When mental health struggles are mistaken for weak faith — creating a devastating double burden.

  • "If I just prayed more, I'd be fine"
  • Shame around seeking professional help
  • Hiding pain from faith communities

Mental struggle is not weak faith. God designed the mind — and healers too.
